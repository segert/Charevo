Beauty

Charevo Fairy Guidance

Fiction:

Imagination:

Femininity:

Name: Beauty
Real Name: Ellie Bellavitz
Behavior Element: Fiction
Mentality Element: Imagination
Identification Element: Femininity
Powers and Abilities:
  • Ability to form weapons and other objects out of her hair
  • Ability to grow and control her hair
  • Ability to shape shift into any female character from TV, movies, and video games
  • As a character, she can summon compatible female characters
  • Ability to transform her wardrobe into however she wants
Weaknesses:
  • Fire and large amount of water in her hair causes her hair powers to be inactive
  • Cannot transform when her arms and hands are restrained

Bio:

Beauty grew up in a small town in Ohio. Her interests weren’t really like that of the other girls in her town, but rather TV, video games, comics, and other forms of pop culture. Because of this, she didn’t really connect with her friends too well, and while they liked how she would dress, with a feminine appearance with her long blonde hair and long skirts, she never really cared how she looked. As a result, Beauty often felt alone when around most people. In addition, while she was seen as looking nice, she thought the most beautiful people were the animated characters she would watch, rather than real women. Her friends all thought she was weird because of that, but she continued to like what she likes, even though she would be the only girl she knew who thought like her. She often felt like she connected more with the female characters she would watch than her actual friends, and would always want to meet these characters, because they do things that no other girl she knows would ever do. The only friend in her hometown who understood her was Tyler Vitti as they both shared a love of pop culture. Beauty and Tyler met when they were eight after she saved him from a bully and they became best friends. At age fifteen, Beauty and Couch Potato both received their powers after watching TV when the town's power plant experienced an accident, briefly affecting the electricity and causing their TVs to have too strong of an image. As they watched for too long, they suddenly gained their abilities. As someone who dreamed of being a superhero, Beauty was ecstatic to see this take place. Beauty and Couch Potato eventually left for Minor City to fight crime like they always wanted, but split up after defeating the Gamer. Not long after, she joined the Neo Brigade.

As one of the few people not originally from a city with anyone who carries an active Charevo Gene, Beauty's only main prior association with superpowers has been through TV and comic books. By not having been familiar with anyone besides Couch Potato with powers, she often gets overly excited about seeing another person use their powers, even if it's a villain attacking her. While she is generally independent and capable of handling a situation by herself, Beauty prefers to be around others so she can share good moments with people. She thinks enjoying success alone is impossible and likes to have friends in her life who she can talk to, which she did not have in Ohio (besides Tyler). By not wanting to go through life alone, she sometimes becomes separate from her own team by not taking things as seriously as them because she treats part of her life like a TV show. While Beauty has embraced her powers, she does resent her Identification Element being Femininity and how she’s seen as just a pretty girl with nothing more to her. Also, because of the fact that she gets electrocuted if someone looks at her when she's not smiling.
