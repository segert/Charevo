Tammy Time

Charevo Fairy Guidance

Tomfoolery:

Monologue:

Attention:

Name: Tammy Time
Real Name: Tammy Masada
Behavior Element: Tomfoolery
Mentality Element: Monologue
Identification Element: Attention
Powers and Abilities:
  • Self-multiplication
  • Ability to inflate her head with air to make it rock solid
  • Ability to float in the air when her head is inflated
  • Ability to duplicate whatever she touches (and have control over the duplicate of a living being)
Weaknesses:
  • Only the original Tammy can clone herself and enlarge her head

Bio:

Growing up, Tammy didn’t have many friends at first and would have several imaginary friends in her toy action figures and would talk to herself through them all the time in her room. This kept her from feeling lonely. Later on, after making a joke about her teacher in school, kids laughed and she felt accepted by everyone. She got in trouble many times, but she loved getting attention and making friends from playing pranks and jokes. Before long, Tammy turned into the class clown so everyone would pay attention to her. As Tammy would continue her antics, some more mature classmates of hers would become tired of her. This made Tammy unhappy that people would disagree with her way of having fun that she wanted to make sure she could get more people to like her. Later, she discovered her powers herself and anything she wanted. When she got her cloning abilities, she was overjoyed that she could make people who would always agree with her. Her clones became her new friends to support her. She even liked making her head bigger so she could get even more attention. She immediately showed off her powers to her friends and used it to play even more tricks on them so she could let everyone know what she could do and be everywhere at once.

One day after school, she used her powers to fool with another kid and Fourize saw her. She was intrigued by Tammy’s powers and her wanting to make trouble for others, so he offered her the chance to join the J Gang as a super villain, saying she’ll get to use her powers to fool with a lot more people to get even more attention. Tammy was happy to see someone who welcomed her mischief, and when she was told she’d get to be with a whole team of kids who’d welcome her mischief, Tammy joined. As a member of the Gang, Tammy is the silly, clever, and mischievous member of the group. Her arch enemy is Bendy because they’re both the jokesters of their teams, but Tammy can get away with more by having more of her in more places whereas Bendy can only be in more places at once, which she uses to rub in Bendy’s face.
